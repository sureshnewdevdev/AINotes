1. Ethical AI and Responsible Development

Ethical AI ensures that Artificial Intelligence is designed and used in a way that benefits humans while minimizing harm. Responsible development focuses on fairness, transparency, accountability, and privacy protection.

Core Principles:

  • Fairness: Avoid bias in training data.
  • Transparency: Explain how models make decisions.
  • Accountability: Keep humans in control of AI outcomes.
  • Privacy: Protect user data responsibly.
